# Meeting Notes

## Technical Issues and Improvements from Recent Testing
William, Gregory, and Nicholas discussed outcomes from the recent testing session, highlighting HUD positioning issues, app timeouts, Deepgram transcription accuracy concerns, speaker detection unreliability, and plans for artifact collection and additional investigation into audio and pause detection.

### HUD Positioning Issues
- William identified problems with HUD/UI positioning during active sessions.
- He will create an issue to track and resolve this.

### App Timeout and Deepgram Connection
- Team encountered app timeouts and a Deepgram connection timeout causing a soft lock.
- Improvements are needed to prevent future lock-ups.

### Transcription Quality and Speaker Detection
- Deepgram word detection was generally decent but occasionally produced errors leading to confusing prompts.
- Speaker detection was unreliable, frequently switching speaker numbers unexpectedly.
- Alternative approaches to speaker calibration may be needed.

### Artifact Collection and Audio Input
- William will set up transcript and audio artifact collection for each test session.
- Team may investigate improving audio input quality, including possibly using a second microphone.

### Pause Detection Challenges
- Deepgram sometimes sends long transcript chunks, triggering prompts during active speech.
- Team is considering adjusting pause duration settings and improving event triggering logic.
